TikTok's system, powered by frameworks like **ByteDance Monolith**, uses a multi-stage process. It starts with a huge pool of videos (**Candidate Generation**), scores them based on your predicted interest (**Fine Ranking**), and finally applies safety and business rules (**Filters**) before delivering the content to your "For You" feed. Your real-time interactions create a constant feedback loop that refines the model instantly.

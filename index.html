The Algorithm Decoded

Understand the multi-stage system that powers the "For You" feed. This section breaks down the journey of a video from upload to potential virality.

The TikTok Recommender Stack

1. Candidate Generation

Initial pool of videos

2. Fine Ranking

Scores based on predicted engagement

3. Re-Ranking & Filters

Applies safety & diversity rules

TikTok's system, powered by frameworks like **ByteDance Monolith**, uses a multi-stage process. It starts with a huge pool of videos (**Candidate Generation**), scores them based on your predicted interest (**Fine Ranking**), and finally applies safety and business rules (**Filters**) before delivering the content to your "For You" feed. Your real-time interactions create a constant feedback loop that refines the model instantly.

High-Weight Ranking Signals

Not all engagement is equal. The algorithm heavily prioritizes signals that indicate deep user satisfaction and content value. Focus your creative energy on these metrics.
